Who I am ?

I'm just someone
who likes to develop websites that convey emotions and offer new experiences to visitors.

What excites me most about being a developer designer is the ability to design and create solutions that have a real purpose and that solve concrete problems. This goes way beyond just designing buttons and websites. My drive involves a passion for creating experiences and solutions that help people, whether by improving their apps, strengthening their online presence, or making their lives easier. To do this, I rely on knowledge of customers and understanding of their needs. I look for relevant problems to solve, deliver solutions quickly and efficiently, learn from them, and then continually iterate and improve that value over time.


When I'm not in front of my computer designing and developing interactive projects, I like to spend time with my wife and son (yes, that's a dog).

What defines me ?

Influencer

I create eye-catching pieces. Not just for their style, but because they reflect the culture they serve. I take the hidden forces that shape our behavior and transform them into powerful results. While many think of "culture" as soccer, coke and what you wear, I know it's much more than that. It's about your beliefs, the companies you patronize and the way you choose them. By diving into the cultural context of each project and understanding the audience's motivations, the stage they're playing on. I'm able to create works that connect in a whole new way. Work that touches people. Work that is culturally charged.

My commitments

As a developer, I look to the future. I'm committed to making a positive impact by working with brands. I also strive to support non-profit organizations in strengthening their impact. I'm aware of the imbalances in our industry and want to change them.

Strategy

I am determined to enrich my experience through teamwork, thus contributing to my daily improvement. The various projects related to web, UX, process, engineering, mentoring, consulting and creative direction fuel my passion, growth and career path.
